· meet the owner ·

Hey guys! I'm Lauren. The creator, owner, & maker of Coco Kini!
I grew up in Corpus Christi, TX. We had family beach days every Sunday for as long as I can remember. Some of my best memories are those of my dad taking us out to the first sandbar and pushing us into crumbly little waves! Fast forward 12 years, I met my fiance` who is full heartedly obsessed with surfing. I honestly had no choice but to really learn how to surf. We started traveling together to beautiful tropical beach destinations like Puerto Rico, Costa Rica, Mexico, New Zealand, and Hawaii. I quickly realized that my bra like tops were not going to cut it in the surf nor were my bottoms. Unfortunately for me I have THE hardest top size ever- as I'm so small around with a larger bust. It was so difficult for me to find good fitting, flattering tops that would hold up in the water and that I felt comfortable in. After a few scares, thinking I lost my bottoms to the ocean I decided I needed to put my sewing skills to the test! It has taken lots of trial and error, and I am continuously working to build and better my skills but this is Coco Kini! It is my goal to provide girls with great fitting, fashionable, functional bikinis that they feel completely confident in, in every situation! -Lauren
